COOKIE POLICY, PIXELS AND TRACKING

Cookies are small text files which contain a small amount of information that is downloaded to user’s computer or mobile device when users visit a website. When users visit the website again, or visit partner website which recognises that cookie, user’s device is able to communicate with the website and the website can read the information held in that cookie.

ReelSaga uses Cookies to help users efficiently browse our Platform and to save user’s time by not having to re-enter user details/preferences on each visit. Cookies allow ReelSaga to provide users with information and show you content relevant to that user. ReelSaga also uses Cookies to analyse how ReelSaga customers interact with ReelSaga Platforms so that ReelSaga can improve the customer journey.

User can choose to accept or decline cookies in its browser settings. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but users can usually modify its browser setting to reject cookies if they prefer. Users may find more help about managing cookies on its browser. This may prevent users from having complete experience of the Platform by affecting the access to some of the links, Services or features. Information collected by cookies and web beacons is Non-Personal Data, unless otherwise constituting as Personal Data under the applicable laws of the applicable jurisdiction, in which case the Personal Data will be handled as per this Privacy Policy.
